Tomattos

The tomato (Solanum lycopersicum) is one of the most commercially important vegetable cultivated worldwide. Tomato is a member of the nightshade family or Solanaceae that includes more than 3000 species. This family also includes other popular species, such as potato, tobacco, peppers, and eggplant (aubergine).

 Rice

Rice is a cereal grain and monocot, a plant with a seed that has one embryonic leaf. It is a member of the grass family, Gramineae (Poaceae).

Ladisfinger

Lady's fingers are a plant in the hollyhock family with mucilaginous seed pods that are edible. They are easy to grow and are a common crop in community gardens.
